# Rapido Kids – Product Management Case Study

A product strategy case study proposing **Rapido Kids**, a dedicated ride experience designed for children with enhanced safety features, guardian controls, and trust-building mechanisms.

## Problem Statement

Parents often hesitate to book bike or cab rides for children due to safety concerns, lack of visibility, and limited guardian controls.

## Solution

Rapido Kids introduces:
- Verified child-safe drivers
- Live guardian tracking
- SOS support
- OTP verification
- Guardian approval
- Child-friendly ride experience
